IndoGlobal Institute of Management, Training And Research (IIMTAR) has been functioning under the aegis of Jagraj Educational and Welfare Society, which was established and registered in New Delhi, India, in the year 2003, under the Societies Registration Act 1860. IIMTAR was formed to empower the student community around the world by imparting professional and management education in order to fulfil the growing need of the global businesses, industries, Trade and commerce for effective and efficient managers, intrapreneurs and entrepreneurs. The academic and the advisory board of the institute comprises of renowned academician, corporate personalities and practitioners from the different sectors of the global economy. The main objective is to assimilate and inculcate managerial skills and traits among the students propelling them to become effective managers, intrapreneurs and entrepreneurs for Global Businesses, Industries, Trade and Commerce.

IndoGlobal Institute of Management, Training And Research (IIMTAR) has also been organising various conferences and seminars in the various domains of business management, Training and Research in association with various institutions, Colleges and Universities. Organising as well as participating in the Conferences and seminars has been a continuous activity at IIMTAR.
